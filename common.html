Signs of stress

Stress comes from stressors such as:

Stressors

The death of a loved one
Injury or illness (oneself or a family member)
Conflict in interpersonal relationships
Feeling unprepared for tests or exams
Financial problems
Class presentations/ public speaking
Fears (fear of flying, heights, meeting new people)
Unrealistic expectations
Lack of social self-confidence
Procrastination and failing to plan ahead
Over scheduling
Conflicts with close friends

Stress can manifest in different ways

Changes in the body

Tense muscles
Rapid breathing and heart rate
Headaches
Difficulties sleeping well
Fatigue

Changes in your behaviours

Withdrawing from others

Fidgeting, feeling restless

Smoking, drinking or using more drugs than usual

Avoiding situations that you think are stressful

Feeling worried or confused

Feeling angry or irritable

Changes in your thoughts

Struggling to concentrate, remember or make decisions
