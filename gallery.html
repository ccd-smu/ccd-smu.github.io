"A bit of stress is good for you"

You probably have heard something like this (usually from your parents when you tell them you're stressed out). However, a bit of stress can actually be good for you.

Eustress
also known as good or positive stress

• Motivates, helps one to focus
• Is perceived as within our coping abilities
• Feels exciting
• Improves performance

Distress
also known as bad or negative stress

• Causes anxiety or concern
• Is perceived as outside of our coping abilities
• Feels unpleasant
• Decreases performance
• Can lead to mental and physical problems

A little bit of stress is not bad

Positive stress could give you energy, motivation and spark creativity; Also makes you more alert and helps you perform better in certain situations.

Stress is only beneficial if it is short-lived.

Excessive or prolonged stress can lead to illnesses such as heart disease and mental health problems such as anxiety and depression.

Good stress
Examples

• Receiving a promotion or raise at work.
• Starting a new job.
• Marriage.
• Buying a home.
• Having a child.
• Moving.
• Taking a vacation.
• Holiday seasons.
• Taking educational classes or learning a new hobby.
