Good/Bad Stress
Next

A little bit of stress is not bad; Positive stress could give you energy, motivation and spark creativity; Also makes you more alert and helps you perform better in certain situations.

Stress is only beneficial if it is short-lived. Excessive or prolonged stress can lead to illnesses such as heart disease and mental health problems such as anxiety and depression.

Eustress
also known as bad or negative stress

Motivates, focuses energy
Is perceived as within our coping abilities
Feels exciting
Improves performance

Distress
also known as good or positive stress

Causes anxiety or concern
Is perceived as outside of our coping abilities
Feels unpleasant
Decreases performance
Can lead to mental and physical problems

Questions to consider when you are assessing for stress:

How do you know when you are stressed?

Where do you feel stress in your body?

What do you notice about your body, thoughts, and feelings when things are difficult?

How do you behave when you are feeling stressed out?

How do you react negatively or proactively to stress?

How do you currently cope with stress?

Did your loved ones or friends make any comments of any change in your body or mood that you might not notice?
