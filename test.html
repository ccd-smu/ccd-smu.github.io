Co-curricular Activities


Embark on your co-curricular activity
Complete your co-curricular activity
Reflect on it (optional but encouraged!)

1

Embark on your co-curricular activity
  • Each co-curricular activity will be tagged to one of the following Graduate Learning Outcomes (GLOs).
  • GLOs form the underlying skills and knowledge that you should pick up and possess in SMU.
  • To know which GLOs your co-curricular activity is/will be tagged to, you may ask your Staff Advisor, or discuss with your peers on what you wish to focus on in your upcoming activity.

2

Complete your co-curricular activity
  • After your activity has been completed, it will be verified and uploaded to the ICMS.
  • You will receive an email notifying you that the record has been created.
  • You will then be able to view the entry on "My SMU Dashboard".
Log in to OnTRAC here
