The ICMS will document the outcomes of your co-curricular experiences, helping you to consolidate your experiences across the co-curricular space in your undergraduate years, and culminating in a co-curricular record that will be meaningful for you and your future employer.

On the ICMS, your community service experiences are organised into Placements, which contain the Pre, Mid & Post Activity requirements as shown.


Each activity that you participate in will have its own Placement entry. As such, if you have more than one CSP/internship/CCA, you will see multiple placements reflected as there will be a Placement for each activity.

  • On the start date of your CSP, you will be prompted to complete your Pre-Activity Learning Objectives (LO) Selection, which is a Survey

  • The learning objectives (LOs) that you choose here set the focus of your experience. You will subsequently be reflecting upon your learning in relation to these identified learning objectives (LOs). These LOs are correspondingly mapped to SMU's Graduate Learning Outcomes.

  • Go into the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio), click on Upcoming or Surveys (on the top bar below the Dashboard header)

  • There, you can view pending Surveys. Click on Take Survey on the Pre-Activity Learning Objectives (LO) Selection that corresponds to your current CSP.

  • Note: Look out for labels such as DUE, OVERDUE & DUE SOON

  • Alternatively, go into the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio), click on Menu (on the top right of the screen)

  • Click on My Placements, and click on the CSP you're active in, and scroll down till you find the Pre-activity selection of LOs (Community Service).

log in to the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio)
  • On the mid-point date of your CSP, you will be prompted to complete your Mid-Activity check-in, which is an Survey

  • Go into the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio), click on Upcoming or Surveys (on the top bar below the Dashboard header)

  • There, you can view pending Surveys. Click on Take Survey on the Mid-Activity Check In that corresponds to your current CSP.

  • Note: Look out for labels such as DUE, OVERDUE & DUE SOON

  • Alternatively, go into the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio), click on Menu (on the top right of the screen)

  • Click on My Placements, and click on the CSP you're active in, and scroll down till you find the Mid-activity Check In (Community Service).

  • Within the Mid-Activity check-in, indicate Yes if you are on track to address the 3 LOs you chose, and write NIL in the comments box.

  • If you wish to amend your LOs, indicate No, and amend them when your write your Post-Activity reflection at the end of your Community Service.

  • If you have other issues with your Community Serivce, please indicate them down in the comments box when doing this Survey, or email your manager.

log in to the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio)

Note: Please complete your reflection within 1 month from the end date of your CSP.


  • On the end date of your CSP, you will be prompted to complete your Post-Activity reflection, which is a Survey

  • Go into the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio), click on Upcoming or Surveys (on the top bar below the Dashboard header)

  • There, you can view pending Surveys. Click on Take Survey on the Post-Activity Reflection that corresponds to your current CSP.

  • Note: Look out for labels such as DUE, OVERDUE & DUE SOON


  • Alternatively, go into the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio), click on Menu (on the top right of the screen)

  • Click on My Placements, and click on the CSP you're active in, and scroll down till you find the Post-activity Reflection (Community Service).

The ICMS will document the outcomes of your co-curricular experiences, helping you to consolidate your experiences across the co-curricular space in your undergraduate years, and culminating in a co-curricular record that will be meaningful for you and your future employer.

On the ICMS, your co-curricular experiences are organised into Placements, which contain the optional Reflection journal as shown.


Each activity that you participate in will have its own Placement entry. As such, if you have more than one CSP/internship/CCA, you will see multiple placements reflected as there will be a Placement for each activity.

Note: Please complete your reflection within 1 month from the end date of your activity.


  • After your CCA experience, you are encouraged to write a reflection, which is a Journal

  • You will be notified by an email from Anthology when your Placement has been created after your CCA experience, within which you may write the reflection

  • Go into the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio), click on Menu (on the top right of the screen)

  • Click on My Placements, and look for the CCA which you have participated in, and scroll down till you find the Journal heading.

  • Click on New Journal Entry. Then, you will be able to choose the appropriate Graduate Learning Outcome (GLO) to reflect upon, and write your refection using the What, So What, & Now What format you've previously learnt in the RPU.
