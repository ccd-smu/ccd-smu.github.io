What is the ICMS?

An introduction to SMU's Integrated Co-curricular Management System

A place to build your experiences

The ICMS documents your co-curricular journey and the learning from these experiences.

Whilst you enjoy and engage in co-curricular activities, the Co-Curricular Transcript offers insights into the skillsets that you've developed outside of the classroom.

Track the progress of your community service, internships, clubs, events and more

Log in to the ICMS > Menu > My SMU Dashboard

Play, for real

Use the ICMS to enhance your co-curricular journey by reflecting on your experiences.

These will help you to talk about your experiences in the future, and enable you to showcase your achievements to impress prospective employers.

Uniquely yours

From 2025, graduating students will be issued a co-curricular transcript alongside the academic transcript.

Apart from simply documenting your activities, your co-curricular transcript will offer insight into the skillsets that you've have developed in the process.

With activities organised under their skills and outcomes instead of names, the transcript is easy to understand

Plan your Community Service Project!

Note: You will only be able to access OnTrac II after your RPU & CSU completion status has been updated. This is updated 6 times a day.

Next update is at

  • Click on Community Service and log in to OnTrac II to apply or propose a Community Service Project (CSP).

  • Source for a CSP, preferably one that allows you to fulfil all your hours; this will give you an opportunity to learn more about the case and beneficiaries you are serving.

  • After applying or proposing, wait for approval from C4SR. Without approval, the community service hours will not be recognised
log in to OnTrac II

Submit your reflections


Complete all 3 mandatory reflections to complete your community service experience, and ensure that it is recorded on your Co-Curricular Transcript.

  • Pre-Activity LO selection
  • Mid-Activity Check in
  • Post-Activity Reflection

Log in to ICMS > Menu > My SMU Dashboard

 


Find the community service entry, click on it, then click on the appropriate Pre, Mid or Post Activity Reflection button

Complete your reflection. Remember to choose only 3 learning outcomes

You'll need to repeat the same steps for your Mid and Post-Activity reflections.

Note: Please complete your Post-Activity reflection within 1 month from the end date of your CSP.

log in to the ICMS (Anthology Portfolio)

Your community service experience(s) will be assessed using the Transformative Learning Rubric and form part of a co-curricular transcript, ready for you when you graduate.

Look for an internship!

Note: You will only be able to access OnTrac II after your RPU & CSU completion status has been updated. This is updated 6 times a day.

Next update is at

  • Click on Undergraduate Career Services and log in to OnTrac II to apply or propose an Internship.

  • Apply for internship opportunities (both local and overseas) posted on ONTRAC II. Alternatively, source for your own internships by submitting a self-proposal on OnTRAC II. Students with internship obligations as part of their scholarship, grants or awards are also required to submit a self-proposal for DKHMCC’s approval.

  • After applying or proposing, wait for an internship offer to be made from the company you have indicated interest in

  • Log on to OnTrac II to accept (or reject) the offer
