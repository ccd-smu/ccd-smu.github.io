Distress vs Eustress
A little bit of stress is not bad; Positive stress could give you energy, motivation and spark creativity; Also makes you more alert and helps you perform better in certain situations.
You probably have heard this before : A bit of stress is good.
But when does it become "bad", if at all?
Well, if it becomes debilitating, to the point that you are not able to perform the functions required in a social setting.
