What is stress?

It is a feeling of

Abnormal Pressure

It can be from different aspects of your day to day life, or your choices.

  

Academic

Unhealthy competition with classmates and peers
High expectations on oneself
Exam anxiety
Impending deadlines

Personal
Building new friendships (New environment/culture)
Balancing social life vis a vis family and school
Managing financial burden
High expectations on oneself
Being overcommitted

  

Work
New environment
Making a good impression
Long hours
Tight deadlines
Increasing demands from boss

etc.

  
Stress
A feeling of being under abnormal pressure.
It can be from different aspects of your day to day life or your choices.
