What can you do?

We know that stress affects all of us, and stressors vary by context, person and situation. Excessive stress, if left unchecked or unmanaged, can put your entire well-being at risk, wrecking havok on your emotional equilibrium as well as physical health.
These are 3 steps you can take if you start feeling stressed.

Remember: we are not endeavouring to eliminate stress completely, but instead learning to manage it effectively

Realise when it is causing you a problem

You need to make the connection between feeling tired or ill, with the pressures you are faced with.

Do not ignore physical warnings such as tense muscles, over-tiredness, headaches or migraine.

!

stress can manifest in various forms

Identify the causes

Sort the possible reasons for your stress into:

1. Those with practical solutions ●
2. Those that will get better given time ●
3. Those you can't do anything about ●

Try to let go of those in the second and third groups- there is no point in worrying.

Review your lifestyle

Are you taking on too much?
Are there things you are doing which could be handed over to someone else?

Can you do things in a more leisurely way?

You may need to prioritise things you are trying to achieve and reorganize your life so that you are not trying to do everything at once.
